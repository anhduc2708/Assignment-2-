    What is the helmet? Helmet is the hat that people wear while they are driving to protect their head. Why should we need to have a smart helmet? What are the advantages of the smart helmet? People are using earphones and airpods to listen to music and answer phone calls while they are driving. The police sometimes will make you paid if you are driving with earphones on. The advantages of a smart helmet are turn on music without your phone outed, answering phone calls, no ropes, and protecting your head. People need to have a smart helmet for not to pay unreasonable purposes and enjoy their hobbies.

    What will the smart helmet look like? The smart helmet will look like the motorbike helmet which will fully cover the user's head. What is special about a smart helmet? Smart helmet contains all of the functions that drivers need while they are driving. It has Bluetooth Operation panel, speaker, upper and downward ventilation system, and many useful functions. People can imagine how amazing is the helmet that can connect to your phone by the Bluetooth Operation system. Drivers can listen to music, answer phone calls while they are driving. The ventilation system will make users feel comfortable while they are driving. The user will not feel hot or mad with the sunshine or hot wind of the summer. The smart helmet also has a windshield to protect the user's face, and also doubles as a screen for the user to look at the google maps. To display an image on the windshield, we use a micro projector, similar to new projecting meter technology inside a modern car. Furthermore, on the forehead area there will be a built in dash cam to record user journey for evidence if there is an acidence, we can extract the video on the mobile app. The helmet will also contain two microphones, one is on the outside and other is inside the helmet. The outside one is for the safety purpose. Because the fully covered helmet kind of muffle the road noise, we may not be able to hear the upcoming vehicles. The microphone will capture the noise and mimic it through a surround headphone inside the helmet. The inside one will be the heart of the system because it will take the user’s voice command and also the microphone that the user will use for answering the phone call. Speaking of the voice command, the user will control every function of the helmet by voice command with the help of built-in Google Assistant AI. There are two ways to charge the helmet, the first way is kind of mundane which just plugs it in the outlet, and the second way is using the sun through the solar power on top of the helmet. Our most proud part of the helmet is the user interface which is applied with Google technology. The Google Assistant is used for taking and answering the commend, If the user needs to use GPS, there is Google Maps. In addition, the user can even update with daily news by the News app of Google.
